About Exam Scheduler

Exam Scheduler is a tool designed to help institutions create optimized exam timetables that prevent overlapping exams for students. With a user-friendly interface and intelligent scheduling algorithms, you can easily upload your exam data, set your preferences, and generate a conflict-free schedule.

This tool was developed as a prototype to demonstrate how automation and optimization can improve the scheduling process.
